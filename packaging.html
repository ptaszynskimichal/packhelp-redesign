Custom Packaging for Your Brand

Packhelp provides businesses with a comprehensive selection of custom packaging solutions. We offer multiple ways to personalise your packaging, from full-colour digital printing to elegant hot-stamping with metallic foils. Add your logo, brand colours, and unique designs directly onto boxes, bags, and mailers. This process gives you direct control over the final appearance of your packaging, ensuring it aligns with your brand identity for e-commerce deliveries, retail sales, or subscription boxes. We handle print-on-demand orders, allowing for flexibility with quantities and designs.

A Complete Range of Packaging Supplies

Our catalogue includes a wide variety of packaging supplies to meet diverse business needs. The range covers everything from rigid cardboard containers to flexible film mailers. We produce structures suitable for shipping heavy items, presenting delicate products, or creating gift sets. This selection includes classic cardboard boxes, lightweight postal bags, and secure carrier bags for in-store purchases. Each category contains multiple sizes and material options, providing a single source for all packaging requirements. Whether for a small online shop or a large retail operation, we stock the necessary components for a complete packaging setup.

Durable Shipping Packaging

For businesses that ship products, our shipping packaging offers security and reliability. We construct our mailer boxes from tough corrugated cardboard, which provides structural integrity during transit. For clothing or non-fragile goods, our poly mailing bags offer a lightweight and water-resistant alternative. We make these bags from durable poly film and they often feature a self-sealing adhesive strip for quick packing. For documents or flat items, we provide a selection of board-backed or bubble-lined envelopes that prevent bending and absorb impacts. We design these solutions to fit within standard courier size limits, helping to manage postage costs effectively.

Versatile Product Packaging

Effective product packaging serves both to protect items and present them professionally. Our range is suitable for direct-to-consumer sales, retail shelves, and subscription services. We use materials like solid and kraft cardboard to create clean, sharp finishes for gift boxes and retail cartons. These materials provide a solid base for high-quality printing. For items like posters, artwork, or cylindrical products, our cardboard packaging tubes offer a purpose-built solution that prevents crushing and creasing. You can select packaging that fits specific product dimensions and enhances the customer's initial interaction with the item.

Customisable Packaging Boxes

Packaging boxes form a core component of our offering, available in numerous styles and configurations. We manufacture boxes from single or double-walled corrugated cardboard for shipping applications, providing robust protection against impacts and compression. For retail and presentation purposes, we use solid cardboard which offers a smoother surface for premium printing. Options include classic shipping cartons, mailer boxes with hinged lids for e-commerce, and two-piece boxes for luxury goods or gift sets. We produce all styles in custom sizes and can print them with your specific artwork.

Flexible Packaging and Accessories

Beyond boxes, we supply a range of flexible packaging formats and essential extras. Our packaging pouch bags work well for small items, samples, or food products, and we supply them in various materials including kraft paper and films. To complete your packaging, explore our packaging accessories category. This includes items such as custom-printed packing tape to seal your boxes securely, tissue paper to wrap products, and void fill to cushion items inside a shipping box. These elements add an extra layer of protection and contribute to a cohesive brand presentation.
